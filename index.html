About Us

AMHSS Thirurkad

AMHSS Thirurkad is a government-aided higher secondary school located in Thirurkad, Perintalmanna, Kerala.

Our school aims to create a conducive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and creativity. Highly qualified and dedicated teachers guide and support students in their educational journey, ensuring they receive a well-rounded education. Apart from academics, AMHSS Thirurkad also provide extracurricular activities and opportunities for students to participate in sports, arts and other co-curricular activities. These activities encourage the development of leadership skills, teamwork, and a sense of social responsibility.
