About Us

AMHSS Thirurkad

AMHSS Thirurkad is a government-aided higher secondary School located in Thirurkad, Perintalmanna, Kerala.

Our School aims to create a conducive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and creativity. Highly qualified and dedicated teachers guide and support students in their educational journey, ensuring they receive a well-rounded education. Apart from academics, Our School also provide extracurricular activities and opportunities for students to participate in sports, arts and other co-curricular activities. These activities encourage the development of leadership skills, teamwork, and a sense of social responsibility.

History

Thirurkad is a remarkable region that pioneered the integration of spiritual and physical education and saw the rise of many institutions in accordance with the modern outlook. Thirurkad AM High School can be described as the mother School of all the institutions here which are working with the aim of academic progress. The forerunners have achieved many invaluable achievements in the rich history of AMHSS.

The history of this School starts from 1921. The institution, which started as a primary School in present Thirurkad town, evolved into a Secondary School in 1964 due to the hard work and dedication of Alavihaji Kolkattil and his Friends. Many of the generations that have graduated from School have become high-ranking and well-known.

This School has taken care to accommodate the new changes and provide appropriate discipline. The achievements of this School are proof of this. The considerable achievements in the curricular and extra-curricular fields have transformed this institution into a popular School. This School was on the verge of success right from the beginning when it experienced a positive change in the field of education. Today AMHSS Thirurkad is one step ahead of neighboring Schools in SSLC Exam Result. On the wings of the 'Vijayabheri' education scheme devised by the District Panchayat, it was able to make a huge improvement in the examination results. AMHSS Thirurkad has received great recognition in the field of Scout & Guide and has received several Rashtrapathi Awards. This success is due to the students who undergo rigorous training and the teachers who are able to guide them. Thirurkad High School is the first School in the district in the field of extra-curricular activities. Success in education and revenue district fairs is proof of this.

Vocational training has helped many students. There are many descendants of this School who left an unforgettable mark in the field of arts, sports and science. The School is proud of its students who have won prestigious scholarships by achieving success in the science aptitude test.

Pace Club is actively working to provide civil service exam training to bright students for implementation of Vijayabheri at the primary level, providing accurate training for competitive exams, subject-wise coaching aimed at various scholarships.

In classes 5 to 10, English medium studies are conducted in separate divisions. Systematic training is imparted in all fields under the leadership of renowned teachers, and this School is a pioneer in IT based studies.
