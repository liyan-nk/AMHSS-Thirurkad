About Us

AMHSS Thirurkad

AMHSS Thirurkad is a government-aided higher secondary school located in Thirurkad, Perintalmanna, Kerala.

Our school aims to create a conducive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, critical thinking, and creativity. Highly qualified and dedicated teachers guide and support students in their educational journey, ensuring they receive a well-rounded education. Apart from academics, Our School also provide extracurricular activities and opportunities for students to participate in sports, arts and other co-curricular activities. These activities encourage the development of leadership skills, teamwork, and a sense of social responsibility.

The school was established in 1921 as a lower primary school. The school was established by Alavi Haji Kolkattil. Elevated as High School in 1964. The Higher Secondary Section granted in 2014 is functioning in the new campus complex. Kamalabhai was the first Principal of the High school.

The school is situated on three acres of land. The high school has 74 classrooms in 10 buildings. Projector and laptop systems set up with the help of Kite Victers in thirty-six classrooms of the high school provide children with audio-visual experiences. High School has 2 computer labs. Hi-tech classrooms and computer labs have been set up by teachers for the UP section.Laptops provided by Kite ensure better computer learning for the students. Broadband Internet facility is available in both labs. The school has a large playground.
